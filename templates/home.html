Smart Emergency Response and Dispatch Platform

A web-based emergency management system designed to improve emergency reporting, location tracking, dispatch monitoring, and emergency response coordination across Nigeria.

Login Register

About The Project

The Smart Emergency Response and Dispatch Platform enables citizens to report emergencies in real time, capture GPS coordinates automatically, track report status, access emergency contacts, and receive emergency response guidelines. The system supports dispatch workflow management and improves emergency coordination between citizens and response agencies.

Key Features

Emergency Reporting

Submit emergency incidents with location, severity level, and description.

GPS Tracking

Capture latitude and longitude automatically for accurate emergency location tracking.

Dispatch Monitoring

Track emergency progress from submission to resolution.

Emergency Contacts

Access important emergency service numbers instantly.
