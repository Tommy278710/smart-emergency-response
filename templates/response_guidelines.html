Emergency Response Guidelines

Fire Emergency

  • Stay calm and alert others nearby.
  • Evacuate the building immediately.
  • Call the Fire Service.
  • Do not use elevators.
  • Move to a designated safe area.

Medical Emergency

  • Call an ambulance immediately.
  • Keep the victim breathing.
  • Provide first aid if trained.
  • Avoid unnecessary movement of the victim.
  • Stay with the victim until help arrives.

Road Accident

  • Secure the accident scene.
  • Contact emergency responders.
  • Do not move seriously injured persons.
  • Direct traffic away from the scene.
  • Wait for professional assistance.

Security Threat

  • Move to a safe location immediately.
  • Contact the Police.
  • Avoid confrontation.
  • Observe and report suspicious activities.
  • Follow instructions from security agencies.

Flood Emergency

  • Move to higher ground.
  • Disconnect electrical appliances.
  • Avoid walking or driving through flood water.
  • Follow evacuation instructions.
  • Monitor official emergency updates.
